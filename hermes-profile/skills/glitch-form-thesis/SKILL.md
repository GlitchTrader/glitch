# Form Thesis

Judge bull, bear, and flat cases from current observable evidence. Aggressive and conservative cases are perspectives, not separate strategies.

1. When flat, describe the most likely next-five-minute path - not the next fifteen minutes - while treating the immediate and next one-minute movement as the primary entry-timing object. When positioned and reviewing each minute, forecast the most likely next one-minute candle and manage from that forecast, structure, and risk. Use 15m/60m as context and opposing-risk awareness, never as permission; use 1m/5m as timing and noise.
2. Consider multiple setup families—continuation, pullback, breakout, mean reversion, scalp, or transition—without requiring or forcing any archetype.
3. Before entry, classify the local move as initiating, progressing, or exhausting. Prefer early participation or a favorable retest; compare a progressing or exhausted move near opposing structure against waiting or remaining flat. Choose `NOTHING` when neither side has positive expectancy with bounded, locally timely structural risk. Ordinary uncertainty, missing order flow, or lack of a closed candle alone is not a veto, but activity, fear of inactivity, and desire for more data are not evidence.
4. Define invalidation before target. Anchor the stop beyond a relevant recent pivot or swing, the actual invalidation, and a volatility/noise buffer - not merely at an offset from immediate price. A valid stop should survive the ordinary one-minute pullback expected by the thesis. Never use `compact`, `tight`, `defined risk`, or an attractive R multiple to place it inside ordinary one-minute movement.
5. Choose a target that the stated horizon, structure, and regime can plausibly reach. Do not select a distant target merely to make reward/risk look good. If valid risk is too large, use a smaller supplied quantity or remain flat.
6. After a stop or failed managed exit, require materially changed price, structure, or immediate behavior before nearby same-direction re-entry and state exactly what changed. A small reclaim or repeated narrative near the same level is churn.
7. For open positions, judge favorable excursion, rollback, and remaining opportunity in initial-risk units and structure. Native stops are catastrophe protection, not passive management.
8. `HOLD` carries the same burden of proof as every other action. When current evidence satisfies the prior review's `change_condition`, do not move the threshold and repeat the same thesis; choose the newly supported action or identify genuinely new evidence that invalidates the old trigger.

Choose `ENTER_LONG`, `ENTER_SHORT`, `HOLD`, `MOVE_STOP`, `MOVE_TP`, `EXIT`, or `NOTHING`. Exact-leg `MOVE_TP` may extend or reduce selected remaining opportunities and may carry an exact-leg stop amendment. Pass only a compact factual `decision_audit`; never reveal private chain-of-thought.
